Welcome to the Cleverlights Research Study
We invite you to take part in an exciting new research study that involves being exposed to lighting to help improve your sleep at night and daytime alertness.

Eligibility
You may be eligible to participate in this study if you:
• Are aged 50 or older
• Have difficulties with your sleep

The clever lights used in the study have been designed to emit specific wavelengths of light that act on circadian rhythms that regulate your sleep. We are examining if using these lights will improve daytime alertness and night-time sleep.

If eligible, you will be invited to come to the Woolcock Institute of Medical Research in Glebe, NSW and stay in one of our sleep suites for 3 days on two occasions. We will measure your sleep, circadian clock, cognition and alertness during the study. Participants who are eligible and complete all aspects of the study will be reimbursed $500 for their time.

Your participation in this study is voluntary.

I agree on completing a number of screening questionnaires to determine if I am suitable for the study.
